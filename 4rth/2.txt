(He eats another forkful of food, then looks thoughtful.) 
JOHN: Has it occurred to you ...? 
SHERLOCK: Probably. 
JOHN: No – has it occurred to you that the bomber’s playing a game with you? The 
envelope; breaking into the other flat; the dead kid’s shoes – it’s all meant for you. 
SHERLOCK (smiling slightly): Yes, I know. 
JOHN: Is it him, then? Moriarty? 
SHERLOCK: Perhaps. 
(The pink phone beeps a message alert. Sherlock switches it on and it sounds two short 
Greenwich pips followed by the longer tone, and a photograph of a smiling middle-aged 
woman appears on the screen.)
